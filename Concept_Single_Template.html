Concept for WP Concept Plugin

Created by Taras Mankovski on December 2nd, 2011

revision

view all

 1

 2

 3

WP Concept Plugin faciliates iterative development of a concept. It allows to invite specific indviduals to comment on a concept. This feedback can be used to create a new revision of the concept. When a new revision is created the audience is notified of the revision and invited to provide further feedback.
